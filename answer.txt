CSP-16 update
Agile prons:
Flexible to changes
Faster feedback from users
Continuous improvement
Higher team collaboration

Agile cons:
Less predictable timeline/cost
Requires strong communication
Scope can expand easily

Non-Agile (Waterfall) – pros
Clear structure and documentation
Predictable schedule and budget
Good for fixed requirements

Non-Agile – cons
Hard to change once started
Late testing/feedback

Scrum Master
Facilitates Scrum process
Removes team blockers
Protects team from distractions
Coaches team on Agile practices

Product Owner
Defines product vision
Manages and prioritizes backlog
Decides what gets built next
Represents customer/stakeholders

Sprint Planning

What will we build this sprint?
How will we do the work?
Daily Scrum (Stand-up)
What did I do yesterday?
What will I do today?
Any blockers?
Sprint Review
Demo of completed work
Stakeholder feedback

Epic – Large feature or goal, broken into many stories
Story – A user-focused feature or requirement
Bug – Something not working correctly
Task – A specific piece of work (technical or general)
